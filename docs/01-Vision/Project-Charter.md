\# NeverLate - Project Charter



\## Project Information



\*\*Project Name:\*\* NeverLate



\*\*Client:\*\* Bladimir Navarro



\*\*Project Owner:\*\* Javier Bello



\*\*Version:\*\* MVP 1.0



\---



\# Executive Summary



NeverLate is a mobile-first application that helps users organize important life events, reminders, documents, payments, medical appointments, and vehicle responsibilities from one secure platform.



The application combines reminders, document management, checklists, and notifications to ensure users are always prepared.



\---



\# Business Problem



Many users miss important appointments or arrive unprepared because documents, reminders, and required items are stored in different places.



NeverLate centralizes everything into a single mobile application.



\---



\# Business Goals



\- Reduce missed appointments

\- Improve personal organization

\- Simplify document management

\- Increase user productivity

\- Build a scalable digital platform



\---



\# MVP Goals



The MVP will validate the following features:



\- Authentication

\- Reminder management

\- Document storage

\- Checklists

\- Push notifications

\- Calendar

\- Statistics



\---



\# Success Criteria



The MVP is considered successful when:



\- Users create reminders successfully.

\- Users upload documents.

\- Notifications are delivered.

\- Checklists are completed.

\- Android and iOS versions are published.
