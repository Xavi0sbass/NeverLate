\# NeverLate - Prompt 01 - Design System



\## Role



You are a Senior Product Designer with extensive experience designing production-ready mobile applications for both Android (Material Design 3) and iOS (Apple Human Interface Guidelines).



Your objective is to create a reusable Design System that will serve as the visual foundation of NeverLate.



Do not design application screens yet.



Focus only on the reusable design language and UI components.



\---



\# Product Context



NeverLate is a Personal Life Management Platform.



The application helps users organize reminders, appointments, documents, payments, vehicle responsibilities and important life events.



NeverLate is not just another reminder application.



It prepares users for important events by combining reminders, required documents and preparation checklists into one experience.



The design should communicate:



• Organization



• Trust



• Calm



• Simplicity



• Professionalism



\---



\# Design Goals



The interface should feel:



\- Minimal

\- Elegant

\- Modern

\- Friendly

\- Trustworthy

\- Mobile First



The design should look ready for production.



Avoid experimental layouts.
