\# NeverLate - Design System



\## Design Philosophy



NeverLate is designed to provide users with a calm, organized, and trustworthy experience.



The interface should reduce stress by making important information easy to find and understand.



\---



\# Design Principles



\- Simple

\- Clean

\- Consistent

\- Accessible

\- Helpful

\- Mobile First



\---



\# Brand Personality



NeverLate should feel:



\- Professional

\- Friendly

\- Calm

\- Reliable

\- Modern
