\# NeverLate - Figma AI Master Prompt



\## Objective



Design a production-ready MVP for NeverLate, a cross-platform mobile application for Android and iOS.



The design must be modern, intuitive, scalable, and ready for Flutter implementation.



\---



\# Product Overview



NeverLate is a Personal Life Management Platform.



It helps users organize important responsibilities by combining reminders, documents, checklists, and notifications into one application.



NeverLate is not simply a reminder application.



It helps users arrive prepared for important life events.



\---



\# Target Audience



Design for:



\- Busy professionals

\- Parents

\- Students

\- Vehicle owners

\- Adults

\- Non-technical users



\---



\# Design Philosophy



The application should feel:



\- Calm

\- Organized

\- Modern

\- Trustworthy

\- Simple



Every screen should reduce stress.



Every interaction should help the user prepare for an event.



\---



\# Visual Style



Inspired by:



\- Material Design 3

\- Apple Human Interface Guidelines

\- Google Calendar

\- Apple Reminders

\- Notion



Avoid clutter.



Use generous spacing.



Rounded components.



Soft shadows.



Modern cards.
